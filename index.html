    • making slides

  • you can code slides in a language called 'MarkDown'
  • it was designed for people to leave comments on pages and make them look nice
  • my cat could use markdown

    • markdown

  • # means top level header i.e. your presentation
  • ## means second level header i.e. a slide
  • ### is third level and so on
  • so start your presentation off with # title
  • then on the next line write ### subtitle
  • then skip a line and write ## first slide title
  • # Office of Transportation Planning at MassDOT
  • ### David Mohler Executive Director
  • ## Section 10: 2009 Transportation Reform Legislation
  • want a numbered list? just start write the list starting with a number followed by a period and a space
  • 1. Establishes an Office of Transportation Planning within MassDOT
  • 2. Serves as principal source of transportation planning for state
  • it doesn't actually matter what the number is
  • 2. Develops state's transportation-related programs outlined in Section 10 of Reform Legislation
  • that turns into

    • Office of Transportation Planning at MassDOT

    David Mohler Executive Director

    Section 10: 2009 Transportation Reform Legislation

    1. Establishes an Office of Transportation Planning within MassDOT
    2. Serves as principal source of transportation planning for state
    3. Develops state's transportation-related programs outlined in Section 10 of Reform Legislation

    with the help of markdown2deckjs

  • want text? just put an emptly line above and bellow a block of text
  • ## OTP Mission
  • Conduct Rigorous, innovative ,and collaborative planning and technology transfer using the best data managment and analysis tools in order to move people and goods on a safe, efficient, and integrated transportation network, comply with federal and state regulations, and support vibrant communities.
  • bullet list? just start it off with an *, or a + or a -
  • want them to come out one by one? end the line with a ~
  • ## Why OTP is the first stop
  • * We provide objective and strategic thinking~
  • + We determine need and focus on an outcome~
  • - We are disciplined and mission driven~
  • this turns into
